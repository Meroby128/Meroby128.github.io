Merve Çoban

I was born in 2002 in Kahramanmaraş, Elbistan, as the second daughter of my family.

I studied at Elbistan Hasan Erdoğan Primary School until the 3rd grade of primary school.

Due to my father's job, I moved to Istanbul with my family.

Secondary School I went to Fatih Secondary School and then Mehmet Niyazi Altuğ Anatolian High School.

And now I'm studying computer engineering at Istanbul Aydin University.
